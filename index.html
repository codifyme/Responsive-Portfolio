About Me

When people say 'responsive design'they mean making your website look good on any device be it a phone, tablet, lapyop or desktop.

in the past, companies tried to use separate websites for mobile device you may recall having to use sites that had an "m."infront of the URL(m.facebook.com) However, this had adverse effect their SEO.

These days, an estimated 70% of a website's traffic comes from mobile devices, and this number is on the rise. in addition, websites that aren't responsive risk losing ground with Google- the company announced that any website that doesnt adapt to mobile screens will lose points in it's search engine ranking.
